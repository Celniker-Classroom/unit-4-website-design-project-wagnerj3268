The whole club comes up with a short film idea and executes the film together. We cover all the basics, separating into groups for actors, editors, filmers, costuming, props, marketing, and more. This way members can get movie making experience in a relaxed group environment with little stress. It also allows for people to focus on whatever area they care about the most, whether people like the business side of film or acting.
