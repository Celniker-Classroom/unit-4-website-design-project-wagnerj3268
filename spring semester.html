Description of what we do in this semester:
Using the skills we learned in the first semester, everyone splits off into groups and makes their own short film. Sometimes there is a theme, other years it will be completely open to any type of short film - whether it be a murder mystery, comedy, or music video! At the end of the semester after films have been finished, submitted, and approved, we host an awards show and film screening where all approved films are shown and attendees vote in various categories for each film. This is a great way to celebrate our accomplishments and share everyone's art!
